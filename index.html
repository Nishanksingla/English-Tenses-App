Simple Present Tense (Present Indefinite)

Positive Form

Sub + V1 ['s' or 'es'] + obj

Negative Form

Sub + do/does + not + V1 + obj

Interogative Form

Do/Does + sub + V1+ obj + ?

Rules

When to use Do/Does:

Do: In Plural sentences
Does: In Singular sentences
In singular sentences, 's' or 'es' is used with verb first form.
In Interogative sentences, when does is used then 's' or 'es' is not used with verb first form.

When to use this tense

जिन हिन्दी वाक्यो का अन्त ता है, ते है, ति है से होता है।

Present Perfect Tense

Positive Form

Sentence Formation Rule:

sub + has/have + V3(past participle)+ obj

Negative Form

Sentence Formation Rule:

sub + has/have + not + V3(past participle)+ obj

Interogative Form

Sentence Formation Rule:

Has/Have + sub + V3(past participle)+ obj + ?

When to use Has/Have:

has: 3rd person singular (he, she, it)
have: all other forms

When to use this tense

जिन वाक्यो का अन्त ता है, ते है, ति है से होता है।
