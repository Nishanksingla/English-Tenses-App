Present Present Tense

Positive Form

Sentence Formation Rule:

Sub + V1 + obj

Negative Form

Sentence Formation Rule:

Sub + do/does + not + V1 + obj

Interogative Form

Sentence Formation Rule:

Do/Does + sub + V1+ obj + ?

Rules

When to use Do/Does:

Do: In Plural sentences
Does: In Singular sentences

When to use this tense

जिन वाक्यो का अन्त ता है, ते है, ति है से होता है।

Tenses

Present Present Tense

Positive Form

Sentence Formation Rule:

sub + has/have + V3(past participle)+ obj

Negative Form

Sentence Formation Rule:

sub + has/have + not + V3(past participle)+ obj

Interogative Form

Sentence Formation Rule:

Has/Have + sub + V3(past participle)+ obj + ?

When to use Has/Have:

has: 3rd person singular (he, she, it)
have: all other forms

When to use this tense

जिन वाक्यो का अन्त ता है, ते है, ति है से होता है।
